All about Volleyball!

What is Volleyball?

Volleyball is a team sport that was first played in 1895 in Holyoke, Massachusetts, United States. In volleyball, there are 2 teams that are separated by a net in the middle of the court. Each team tries to score points by grounding a ball on the other team's court.

Rules

Here are some basic rules to follow when playing volleyball...

- 6 players on a team, 3 on the front row and 3 on the back row.
- Maximum of three hits per side.
- A player CANNOT hit the ball 2 times in a row.
- Ball may be played off the net during a volley and on a serve.
- The ball hitting a boundary line is "in".
- Call "mine" when going for the ball, even when it seems obvious
- Have fun!

Equipment

In volleyall, there isn't a lot of equipment needed. All you need is a volleyball, a net, and proper shoes to play in. If you dive a lot in volleyball, I recommend getting knee and shoulder pads so you don't hurt yourself in a game!
